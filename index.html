Permanent Collection

Journey through 4.6 billion years of Earth's history with our extensive permanent collection featuring fossils, minerals, and specimens from around the world.

Paleontology Hall

Discover prehistoric life through our collection of dinosaur fossils, including our famous T-Rex skeleton and Triceratops display.

Mineral Gallery

Marvel at stunning gemstones, crystals, and rare minerals from every continent, including our prized meteorite collection.

Wildlife Dioramas

Experience diverse ecosystems through lifelike dioramas showcasing animals in their natural habitats.

Current Exhibits

Featured Exhibit

Mysteries of the Deep Ocean

Dive into the unexplored depths of our oceans and discover creatures that have never seen sunlight. This immersive exhibit features bioluminescent displays, deep-sea specimens, and interactive submarine experiences.

Now through December 2025

Temporary Exhibit

Climate Through Time

Explore how Earth's climate has changed over millions of years and understand the current climate challenges. Interactive models show ice age cycles, extinction events, and future predictions.
